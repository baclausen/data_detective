Chapter 1: The Mysterious Missing Data
Once upon a time, there was a detective named Data who loved to solve puzzles...
Suddenly, a mysterious figure left behind a clue: a USB drive labeled "TOP SECRET".
Data opened it, only to find encrypted files that hinted at a hidden treasure...

The drive may have contained malware...

Chapter 2: The Hacker
To discover the source of the code, Data scours the dark web in search of an old nemesis.

Chapter 3: City Blackout
Just as they schedule a time and place to meet, a countdown shows on his screen and the whole city goes dark.
